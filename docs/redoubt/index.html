02The problem

The cloud was a fine place to start. It's a poor place to live.

Three things tend to surface once an AI workload stops being a pilot.

Data leaves the building

Every inference call is a round trip through someone else's infrastructure, policy, and incident report.

Costs follow usage, not budgets

Token meters don't pause for your fiscal year, and neither does the invoice.

The roadmap isn't yours

Model deprecations, rate limits, and pricing changes arrive on someone else's schedule.

03Capabilities

What we build inside your walls

Four disciplines, one accountable team.

Hardware & deployment

Sourcing, racking, and commissioning compute sized to the workload you actually have, not a vendor's minimum order.

Model orchestration

Running, versioning, and routing open and licensed models across your own cluster, with no update arriving uninvited.

Security & isolation

Network segmentation, encryption at rest and in transit, and access control built around the compliance you already answer to.

Monitoring & support

Live visibility into utilization, latency, and health, with a team that picks up when something needs attention.
