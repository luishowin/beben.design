Philosophy

The best coffee brands don't shout.
They invite.

We believe a cafe's brand should feel like the first sip of a well-made cup — warm, considered, and worth lingering over. Born from years behind the espresso machine, this studio exists at the intersection of design craft and coffee craft.

We don't design from mood boards and trend reports. We design from the smell of freshly ground beans at 5am, from the sound of milk stretching, from the quiet pride of a barista who got the pour just right. That's the feeling your brand should carry.

The Barista Behind the Brand

Onesmus Kimani

“I didn't start at a design desk. I started behind a machine.”

Before I ever picked up a design tool, I was dialing in grind sizes and pulling shots. Years of making coffee — and watching what makes people stay — taught me what most designers never learn: a brand isn't what you see. It's what you feel when you walk through the door.

I started Dark Roast Arabica Studio because I kept seeing cafes with incredible coffee and forgettable brands. They deserved better. They deserved someone who understood both the craft in the cup and the craft on the screen.

That's what this studio is — design from someone who still pulls shots on the weekends.
