01 Design Concept & Ethos

Built on
principles.
Not trends.

The Outpost strips micro-living down to its honest skeleton — ISO-compliant dimensions, explicit load paths, and every component designed to be replaced, not discarded. Buildable by a small team. Scalable from V1 to V3.

  • Function over excess
  • Structural honesty — load paths are explicit
  • Modularity first — everything replaceable
  • Self-sufficiency within reason
  • Buildability by small teams
  • Long-term serviceability

03 Integrated Systems
SYS — 01
Power

Five 400W panels feeding a 20 kWh LiFePO4 bank through a 5 kW inverter. Grid bypass ready. 240V AC distribution throughout. Sized for full-time off-grid operation.

2 kW Solar · 20 kWh Battery
SYS — 02
Water

100L rooftop tank, gravity-fed or pump-assisted. External quick-connect inlet for top-up. Black and grey water separated at source and routed for reuse.

100L Tank · Gravity + Pump
SYS — 03
Control

Central monitoring unit tracks door and window status, tap state, and power system metrics. Barn Door Mode deploys all security grills simultaneously and locks down the unit.

Sensors · Power Monitor · Lockdown
